Bio

Hello! I'm Kai Zheng, a 4th year Ph.D. candidate at University of California San Diego, advised by Professor Xinyu Zhang. Before pursuing my Ph.D. degree, I worked as an engineer for Pi-Radio Inc., a spin-off from NYU WIRELESS. I received my master's degree from New York University and my bachelors' degree from Fudan University.

My research interests lies in wireless sensing and communication, with a focus on mmWave sensing/communciation, low-power Internet of Things (IoTs) and neuromorphic wireless sensing.
