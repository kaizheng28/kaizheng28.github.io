Biography

Hello! I'm Kai Zheng, a 4th year Ph.D. student at University of California San Diego, advised by Professor Xinyu Zhang . I received my master's degree from New York University and my bachelors' degree from Fudan University.

My research interest includes mmWave communication, wireless sensing and neurmorphic computing.
